Have you ever heard about the sunfish? It is one of the largest bony fish in the world, weighing up to 4,400 pounds.. It looks like a sideways fish fused with a giant pancake. This creature went viral at one point, because somebody posted a rant about how useless they are. The rant has a lot of outdated info and misinformation, but still managed to become very popular. This actually caused people to despise the endangered sunfish, even killing them unnecessarily.
